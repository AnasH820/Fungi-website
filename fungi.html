Fungi 1.2

Competitive jumping game where survival depends on skill and strategy. Gain Fungus Points, defeat enemies, and climb the leaderboard!

Description

Fungi is a competitive game where survival depends on jumping over enemies. When you jump on an enemy, you gain Fungus Points. This number appears above your character and represents your health. Enemies also have Fungus Points, and they gain or lose them the same way—by jumping on you or on each other. Rule is simple: The more enemies you jump on, the longer you survive.

How to Play

  • Jumping on enemies increases your Fungus Points.
  • Getting jumped on decreases them.
  • When an enemy’s Fungus Points reach zero, it dies.
  • If your Fungus Points reach zero, you die too.
  • Fungus Points also give you XP.
  • As your XP increases, your level increases.
  • Every new level unlocks new enemies, increases difficulty, decreases spawn time, and increases the number of enemies.
  • If you die, you can revive by paying 5 Gems (keeps your highest Fungus Points and XP).
  • Skins affect your initial health, speed, jump height, and special abilities (double jump, fire, snow, resistance).
